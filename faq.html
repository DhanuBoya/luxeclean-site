Frequently Asked Questions

What’s included in a standard clean?

Everything you need for a guest-ready space: floors, surfaces, bathrooms, kitchens, and bins.

Do I need to be home during the clean?

Not at all. Most of our hosts use key boxes or digital locks for seamless entry.

Can I book recurring cleans?

Yes – we support weekly, fortnightly, or custom schedules. Just mention it during booking or reach out via Contact.
