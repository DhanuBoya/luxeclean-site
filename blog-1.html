First Impressions Matter

Your guest walks through the door. What do they see? Smell? Touch? A spotless countertop. Fluffy, fresh towels. Not a single dust speck in sight. This isn’t just cleaning — it’s the art of hospitality. And it’s the difference between a 4-star and a 5-star review.

The Cleaning Details That Guests Actually Notice

  • Sparkling Bathrooms: No mold, no streaky mirrors, no soap scum.
  • Crisp Linens: Always go with fresh sheets and pillowcases.
  • Odour-Free Everything: Lingering smells = instant mood killer.
  • Kitchen Magic: Even unused appliances must shine.
  • Floors You Could Eat Off: Mop until reflective. Vacuum corners.

Quick LuxeClean Pro Tip:

Use triple sheeting — a method used in luxury hotels. Hygienic, elegant, and fast to turn over.

Small Touches = Big Love

Guests rave about:

  • Rolled towels or folded corners
  • Scented diffusers (subtle!)
  • A personal welcome note
