About LuxeClean

More than just a cleaning service — we're your partner in hospitality excellence.

Our Story

LuxeClean Melbourne was born from a simple observation: short-stay rentals deserve hotel-grade cleaning without the hotel prices. Founded by local professionals who’ve hosted, cleaned, and managed properties themselves, we know exactly what Airbnb guests expect — and what hosts need to earn 5-star reviews consistently.

Our Mission

To empower Airbnb hosts and property managers with seamless, on-demand, premium cleaning — the kind that turns guests into repeat customers.

Why We’re Different

  • Specialized in Airbnb & short-stay cleans
  • Transparent pricing. No hidden fees. Ever.
  • Fast, reliable, and powered by smart tools like Calendly & live chat
  • Every clean is reviewed — we aim for “wow” not just “clean”
